Code School Profiles:

Why I'd love to join Founders and Coders:

I’m a postdoctoral scientist, currently trying to understand how cancer becomes resistant to different drugs, and how we might overcome this. A large part of my work is computational analysis, trying to integrate different genomics data into our models. I’m naturally curious, and love analysing big datasets to try to make sense of it all. I’m also very considered; I always weigh up both sides. And lastly, my values are really important to me; I’ll always try to do what’s right.

Why do I want to join F&C?

I want to build apps that help people to do good science. I really enjoy the computational side of data analysis, and I can now see lots of exciting opportunities where fullstack development skills would really enable me to overcome challenging problems in science. Further, my Facet5 personality type is a ‘coach’; so giving back and mentoring others is a really important part of who I am.

What motivates me?

That’s easy. I love challenging, ambitious projects.. And projects that make a difference to the world.

What do I want to do next?

I want to combine my Data Science skills, my expertise in genomics, and software development skills to engineer creative solutions to some of the big data challenges that scientists are facing. The field is producing huge amounts of data, but issues in storing, accessing, sharing, visualising and analysing these datasets are a massive bottleneck. There are numerous small start-ups, and even large companies such as Google are working on this, but they’re all in need of skilled developers with good technical understanding of genomics, and I think I’ve got a huge amount to offer in this area.
